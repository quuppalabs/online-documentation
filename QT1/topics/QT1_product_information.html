Product Information

This section includes the technical specifications as well as packaging and storage information for the device.

Features

The Quuppa QT1 has the following features:

  • Button

  • Red LED

  • 2.4 GHz radio SoC

  • Accelerometer

  • 17 I/Os of which 8 are usable with normal Quuppa Tag Firmware and rest with the Quuppa Tag Firmware Library.

  • Compatible with Quuppa FW and library

  • Pre-programmed with Quuppa FW

Technical Specifications

Item Description
Dimensions 26.6 mm × 24.5 mm × 1.7 mm
Weight 1.5 g
Operating temp. range −30°C ... +85°C
Storage temp. range −30°C ... +85°C

2401 2402 … 2408 2481 MHz

Certifications CE, FCC, IC, Japan, Korea, Saudi Arabia and Brazil
Functionality Button, LED, Accelerometer, I/O pins for connecting simple peripherals, compatible with Quuppa firmware library
Important: Quuppa devices should only be used in environments that fit the conditions listed in the above table. Exposure to more stress (e.g. operating the device in higher than recommended temperatures) may affect the reliability of the device or even cause permanent damage to the device.

Packaging and Storage

The QT1 is shipped in a plastic tray made of ESD safe material. One tray contains up to 20 QT1.

Please note the storage temperature ranges when storing the QT1. The device should be stored in a dry environment as humidity may damage the device. Please note that the device is sensitive to ESD and may be damaged by it.
