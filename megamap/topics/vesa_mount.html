VESA Mount

SINGLE ARM MOUNT WITH TILT

Length:

  • 190 mm (straight)

  • 80 mm (bent)

Weight: 1.26 kg

Max load: 20 kg

Includes

  • Screws (two lengths, 4 pcs each)

  • Cable holder (for Ethernet cable)

How to use: Q35 mounting options

  1. Straight - upwards to ceiling (190 mm)

  2. Bent - upwards to ceiling (80 mm)

  3. Bent - wall/corner

  4. Tilted - wall/ceiling
