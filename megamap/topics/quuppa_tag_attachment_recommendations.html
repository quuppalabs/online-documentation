Quuppa tag attachment recommendations

The main objective when selecting the placement of the tag is to maximise the line of sight to as many locators as possible.

Note that at 2.4Ghz, the human body is not a good transparent medium and will block the signal. Please consider the following recommended locations when attaching Quuppa tags to the human body, especially sports players.

Not recommended

The central part of the body blocks the radio signal from being seen by the locators. Hence, tags should not be attached around the waste or chest.

Recommended

Locations for attaching the tags are:

  • helmet
  • shoulders or shoulder pads
  • limbs: wrist, ankle, lower legs

Sub-optimal

This is a feasible placement, but the tag may suffer from partial obstruction. The system could run optimally.

NOTE: Quuppa QT1-1 and QT1 do not fulfill the requirements of Article 3(3), point (e) of EU Directive 2014/53/EU (RED Delegated Act on cybersecurity) if used for people tracking with user registration and/or the device is routinely carried by the same person.
