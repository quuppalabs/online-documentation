Quuppa Development Kit Quick Start Guide

Thank you for purchasing a Quuppa Development Kit

This Development Kit is for evaluating the Quuppa Intelligent Locating System™, studying system integration feasibility, and building software applications and services for demonstration purposes. At this point, we presume you have completed the online e-learning academy. If you have not already completed the e-learning academy, please contact Quuppa Support to request access.

This Quick Start Guide will walk you through the basic steps of setting up your first project using:
  • Quuppa Site Planner (QSP)
  • Quuppa Customer Portal (QCP)
  • Quuppa Positioning Engine (QPE)

There will be a lot of new concepts and terminology along the way. Don't worry, you don't need to master them all right away. Further information will be waiting on the Quuppa Documentation webpage (http://www.quuppa.com/documentation) once you are ready.
