Q-Port 8 Product Specification

A PoE-powered cable concentration device to help reduce the cost of cabling, installation & deployment.

General

Power (via PD Port) Input: Ultra PoE or IEEE802.3af/at compliant

PoE Budget: 95 watts (maximum)
Package Contents
  • 8-Port PoE powered cable aggregation device
  • DIN-rail mount bracket
  • Instructions

Features

LEDs
  • PoE
  • Power
  • Link/activity PoE
Connection Provides up to seven Locators with power and data
Connection Distance Doubles the connection distance between PoE source and device from 100 m (328 ft.) to 200 m (656 ft.)

Mechanical

Dimensions 108mm x 138mm x 50mm
Weight 673 g
Mounting Options

DIN-mount

Environmental & Regulatory

Operating Temperature -20 – 70℃ (-4 – 158°F)
Storage Temperature -40 – 75°C (-40 – 167°F)
Operating humidity: 10 – 90% RH, non-condensing
Storage humidity: 5 – 90% RH, non-condensing
Certifications FCC Class A, CE, RoHS

Rated for shock (IEC 60068-2-27), freefall (IEC 60068-2-32) and vibration (IEC 60068-2-6)
Other Green Ethernet power-saving technology deactivates unused ports and adjusts power levels based on the cable length
