QC6 Quuppa Controller Product Specification

The QC6 Quuppa Controller is a preconfigured device for running the Quuppa Intelligent Locating System™. The system allows you to locate and track any objects equipped with Quuppa Tags in an environment where Quuppa Location infrastructure, including our Locators, has been installed.
