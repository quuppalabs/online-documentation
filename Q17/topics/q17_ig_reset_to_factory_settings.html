Reset to Factory Settings

To reset to factory settings, press the reset button while connected to a power supply and keep it pressed. The red indicator light flashes for a moment, then stays on for a moment and then turns off. Now the button can be released. After that, the Quuppa Q17 switches on normally with the default factory settings.
